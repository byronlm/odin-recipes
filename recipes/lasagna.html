Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty and delicious Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Baked until golden and bubbling, each slice offers a satisfying blend of savory flavors and comforting textures. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, lasagna is a timeless favorite that brings people together around the table.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato sauce or marinara
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Egg (for mixing with ricotta)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil or Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Simmer for 15–20 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg and a bit of salt.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, then a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of ricotta mixture and shredded mozzarella.
  8. Repeat the layers until ingredients are used up, finishing with sauce and a generous sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 20 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  10. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.
